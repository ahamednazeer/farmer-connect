At Farmer Connect, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Quick Summary: We collect information to provide better services, we don't sell your data, and we use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register and use our platform, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, username, password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, bio, location, farming details (for farmers)
  • Contact Information: Delivery addresses, emergency contact details
  • Verification Information: Government ID, farming certificates, business documents
Transaction Information
  • Order details, payment information, transaction history
  • Communication between buyers and sellers
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Shipping and delivery information
Technical Information
  • IP address, device information, browser type
  • Usage data, log files, cookies
  • Location data (with your permission)
  • Performance and analytics data

2. How We Use Your Information

Core Services
  • Create and manage your account
  • Process orders and payments
  • Facilitate communication
  • Verify farmer credentials
  • Provide customer support
Platform Improvement
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve our services
  • Personalize recommendations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Ensure platform security
Communication
  • Send order confirmations and updates
  • Notify about account activities
  • Share promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Send security alerts
  • Provide customer support

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent
  • When you explicitly agree to share information
  • For promotional activities you've opted into
Service Providers
  • Payment processors for transaction processing
  • Delivery partners for order fulfillment
  • Cloud service providers for data storage
  • Analytics providers for platform improvement
Legal Requirements
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to legal process or government requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

Technical Safeguards
  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Secure servers and databases
  • Regular security audits
Administrative Safeguards
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Employee training on data privacy
  • Background checks for staff
  • Incident response procedures
Note: No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Enable basic functionality, maintain sessions Session/1 year
Performance Analyze usage, improve platform performance 1-2 years

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request a copy of your personal information we hold.

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate personal information.

Deletion

Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements).

Objection

Object to certain processing of your information.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@farmerconnect.com or through your account settings.

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites or integrate with third-party services:

  • Payment Gateways: We use secure payment processors for transaction handling
  • Social Media: Integration with social platforms for sharing and login
  • Analytics: Third-party analytics tools to understand platform usage
  • Delivery Partners: Integration with logistics providers for order delivery
Important: These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies before using their services.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you are under 18, please do not provide any information on our platform.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information from our records.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you via email or platform notification for significant changes
  • Provide at least 30 days notice for material changes
  • Maintain previous versions for your reference

Your continued use of our platform after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
